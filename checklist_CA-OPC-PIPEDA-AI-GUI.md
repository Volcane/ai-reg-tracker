# PIPEDA AI Compliance Checklist for Healthcare Organizations

## 1. Immediate Actions (within 30 days)

- [ ] Conduct inventory of all AI systems currently processing patient personal information (including diagnostic AI, predictive analytics, chatbots, and administrative AI tools)
- [ ] Identify all personal health information being used to train, test, or validate AI models
- [ ] Review existing patient consent forms to determine if they cover AI processing activities
- [ ] Assess current privacy policies for gaps related to automated decision-making disclosure requirements
- [ ] Document all third-party AI vendors and service providers currently processing patient data
- [ ] Establish temporary data processing restrictions for AI systems lacking proper PIPEDA consent
- [ ] Notify Privacy Commissioner if any AI-related privacy breaches occurred in past 12 months that weren't previously reported
- [ ] Create cross-functional AI governance team including Legal, IT, Clinical, and Privacy roles

## 2. Near-Term Actions (31–90 days)

- [ ] Update patient consent forms to explicitly cover AI training, testing, and deployment using their personal health information
- [ ] Develop AI-specific privacy impact assessments (PIAs) for each system processing personal data
- [ ] Create patient-facing documentation explaining how AI systems make automated decisions affecting their care
- [ ] Implement technical measures to track and log AI decision-making processes for transparency requirements
- [ ] Establish data processing agreements with AI vendors that include specific PIPEDA compliance clauses
- [ ] Update privacy policies to include detailed sections on AI processing, automated decision-making, and patient rights
- [ ] Develop procedures for patients to request explanations of AI-driven decisions affecting their healthcare
- [ ] Create vendor due diligence questionnaire specifically addressing PIPEDA AI guidance requirements
- [ ] Implement consent withdrawal mechanisms for patients who no longer want their data used in AI systems
- [ ] Train clinical and administrative staff on new AI transparency and consent requirements

## 3. Ongoing Obligations (recurring compliance tasks)

- [ ] **Monthly**: Review AI system logs to ensure automated decisions are properly documented and explainable
- [ ] **Quarterly**: Audit AI vendor compliance with PIPEDA requirements and data processing agreements
- [ ] **Quarterly**: Update AI system inventory and assess new privacy risks from system changes or updates
- [ ] **Semi-annually**: Review and update AI-specific consent mechanisms based on new use cases or regulations
- [ ] **Annually**: Conduct comprehensive PIPEDA compliance assessment for all AI systems processing personal data
- [ ] **Annually**: Update privacy impact assessments for AI systems and document any changes to processing activities
- [ ] **Annually**: Train staff on PIPEDA AI guidance updates and organizational policy changes
- [ ] **As needed**: Conduct privacy impact assessments before implementing new AI systems or significantly modifying existing ones
- [ ] **As needed**: Respond to patient requests for AI decision explanations within PIPEDA's required timeframes
- [ ] **As needed**: Update vendor assessments when onboarding new AI service providers

## 4. Documentation Required

- [ ] Complete inventory of AI systems with detailed data flow mapping showing personal information processing
- [ ] Consent records demonstrating valid PIPEDA consent for AI training and deployment activities
- [ ] Privacy impact assessments for each AI system processing personal health information
- [ ] Data processing agreements with AI vendors including specific PIPEDA compliance obligations
- [ ] AI decision-making process documentation enabling meaningful explanations to patients
- [ ] Vendor due diligence records demonstrating PIPEDA compliance assessment for AI service providers
- [ ] Training records showing staff education on PIPEDA AI guidance requirements
- [ ] Patient communication records for AI decision explanations and consent withdrawal requests
- [ ] Incident response documentation for any AI-related privacy complaints or breaches
- [ ] Regular compliance audit reports assessing PIPEDA adherence across AI systems
- [ ] Policy documents covering AI-specific privacy practices and automated decision-making procedures
- [ ] Technical documentation showing accountability measures and transparency controls in AI systems

## 5. Team Responsibilities

### Legal Team
- [ ] Review and update all AI-related contracts with vendors for PIPEDA compliance
- [ ] Develop consent language and patient rights procedures for AI processing
- [ ] Respond to Privacy Commissioner inquiries related to AI systems
- [ ] Update organizational privacy policies for AI-specific requirements

### Privacy/Compliance Team
- [ ] Conduct privacy impact assessments for AI systems
- [ ] Monitor ongoing PIPEDA compliance across AI implementations
- [ ] Handle patient requests for AI decision explanations
- [ ] Coordinate with Privacy Commissioner on AI-related matters

### IT/Engineering Team
- [ ] Implement technical controls for AI system transparency and accountability
- [ ] Maintain AI system logs and decision-making documentation
- [ ] Configure consent management systems for AI-specific processing
- [ ] Ensure data security measures meet PIPEDA requirements for AI systems

### Clinical/Medical Team
- [ ] Provide clinical context for AI decision-making transparency requirements
- [ ] Participate in patient communication about AI-driven healthcare decisions
- [ ] Validate that AI explanations are clinically accurate and meaningful
- [ ] Report AI-related patient concerns to privacy team

### Procurement/Vendor Management Team
- [ ] Conduct PIPEDA compliance due diligence for AI vendor selection
- [ ] Negotiate data processing agreements with AI service providers
- [ ] Monitor ongoing vendor compliance with PIPEDA AI requirements
- [ ] Manage vendor risk assessments for AI systems processing personal data

### Quality Assurance/Risk Management Team
- [ ] Audit AI systems for PIPEDA compliance on regular schedule
- [ ] Assess privacy risks from AI system changes or updates
- [ ] Document compliance gaps and remediation activities
- [ ] Report AI privacy risks to executive leadership and board
